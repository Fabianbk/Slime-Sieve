# SlimeSieve
Bring the classic **Ex Nihilo** Skyblock experience to your Slimefun server! **SlimeSieve** is a Slimefun4 Addon that introduces early-game resource generation mechanics, allowing players to smash, sift, and process their way to riches. Works best with [SaneCrafting](https://blob.build/project/SaneCrafting) Addon.

## Features

* **Hammers (4 Tiers):** Smash your way from Cobblestone -> Gravel -> Sand -> Dirt. Features a dynamic tool-swapping system (Paxel behavior) that automatically switches between a Pickaxe and a Shovel depending on the block you are hitting! Available in Wooden, Stone, Iron, and Diamond tiers.
* **Crook & Silkworms:** Need string early on? Use a Crook to break leaves and find **Silkworms**. Right-click a leaf block with a Silkworm to infest it (turning it into a Cobweb). Break the infested leaves with your Crook to harvest String! The Crook dynamically swaps to a Sword when breaking cobwebs for lightning-fast harvesting.
* **The Sieve & Meshes:** Sift Dirt, Gravel, and Sand using an interactive Sieve block. Craft and install 4 tiers of Meshes (**String, Flint, Iron, Diamond**) to unlock better drop tables.
* **Ore Processing Pipeline:** Sifting yields **Ore Pieces** (Iron, Gold, Copper, Aluminum, Lead, Silver). Combine 4 identical Ore Pieces in an Enhanced Crafting Table to create an **Ore Chunk**. Finally, smelt the Ore Chunks in the Slimefun **Smeltery** to extract pure Ingots. Or put Iron, Gold, Copper in the vanilla furnace to get vanilla ingot.

## How to Play (The Early Game Loop)

1. Craft a **Crook** and break some tree leaves to gather **Silkworms**.
2. Right-click leaves with a Silkworm to infest them. Wait 3 seconds, then break the resulting cobweb with your Crook to get **String**.
3. Craft a **Sieve** and a **String Mesh**, then install the mesh into the Sieve (Right-click).
4. Craft a **Hammer** and smash Cobblestone to generate **Gravel** and **Sand**.
5. Hold Gravel, Sand, or Dirt in your main hand and Right-click the Sieve to start sifting for resources and Ore Pieces!
6. Upgrade your Meshes and Hammers as you progress to increase your efficiency and unlock rarer drops like Diamonds and Emeralds.

## Installation

1. Download the latest `SlimeSieve-vX.X.X.jar` from the [Releases](https://github.com/Fabianbk/Slime-Sieve/releases/tag/v1.0.0) page.
2. Place the `.jar` file into your server's `plugins/` folder.
3. Ensure you have the latest version of [Slimefun4](https://github.com/Slimefun/Slimefun4) installed.
4. Restart your server and enjoy!
